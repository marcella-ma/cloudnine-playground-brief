01 · Overview

The concept

Grounding is the product universe, but the hoodie stays the constant. Every color drop is a new chapter and a new lens on what "grounded" means. Same word, new angle, same hoodie.

Strategically, this compounds one anchor keyword ("Grounding") for SEO, Pinterest, and brand recall, while training the community to read the brand as a recurring system instead of one-off restocks. Easter eggs across chapters — recurring motifs, callbacks in captions, color carryovers — reward the people who pay attention.

Playground is about reconnecting with the inner child and the feelings a playground brings, no matter your age. A ground to play on. A ground to play with.

Main Product

Grounding Hoodie

Not a new product, just new colors. Four new colorways: Mesa Rose, Riviera, Shale, Pale Olive.

The objective

Campaign Objective

Reintroduce the Grounding Hoodie through four new colorways and open the Grounding Series — establishing color drops as chapters with meaning, not restocks.

Campaign Goal

Retention

NCAC is strong. Retention is the gap. This drop is built to bring the existing community back.

Business Goals

  • Drive repeat purchase
  • Increase LTV
  • Hold AOV (no margin erosion)

Brand Goals

  • Grounding becomes a signature product, not a restocked SKU
  • Launch the chapter system so the community starts reading the brand
  • Build Pinterest/SEO equity around "Grounding"
  • Add visual depth to the brand world — playground assets feed for months

05 · Campaign Offer

Access, not discount

No public discount. No price change on the new colors. The offer is access — with community-only pricing for insiders.

Tier 1 · Insiders
Discord Community
Members get an announcement days before, then an exclusive discount code valid 48h before public launch. Reactivates Discord engagement.
48h Early Access · Discount Code
Tier 2 · Subscribers
Newsletter Sign-ups
Email subscribers get a calendar invite for May 28 a week before. 10% off for the launch window.
Calendar Invite · 10% Off
Tier 3 · Public
Public Launch
May 28. Open to all. Full price. The chapter system itself creates the pull.
Open Shop · Full Price
Why this works

No public discount protects margin and avoids training the community to wait for sales. The Discord tier reactivates an under-performing channel by giving it the most valuable access. Every Tier 2 + 3 purchase auto-enters "The Cloud" — meaning next chapter, they're in Tier 1.
